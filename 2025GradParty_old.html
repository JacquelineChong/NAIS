The Eighth at Hong Kong Jockey Club offers an elegant and sophisticated setting for our graduation celebration. Located on the 8th floor of The Jockey Club New Clubhouse in Happy Valley, this premium venue provides stunning views and a luxurious atmosphere.

Your fee includes:

  • Professional photography services at the party
  • Complimentary for Year 6 teachers, TLAs, Mr Duncan, Mrs Harley and the Primary Leadership Team
